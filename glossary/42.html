question：A bank is planning to introduce a new term deposit service. It is predicted that the volume of deposits is directly proportional to the deposit rate, with a proportional constant k (where k > 0). So, ( h(x) = x cdot g(x) ). Substituting ( g(x) ) from above, that would be ( h(x) = x cdot kx = kx^2 ).
